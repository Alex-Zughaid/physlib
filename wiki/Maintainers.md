# Maintainers

The default policies around the maintainer team follow Mathlib's, which are
the fall-back wherever there's conflict or disagreement.

## Role

1. Give final reviews of pull requests.
2. Help structure and guide the project.
3. Help foster a pleasant and active community around the project.

## Becoming a maintainer

Maintainers have research-level knowledge of physics or mathematics, and
experience with either the Physlib or Mathlib review process. Becoming one
requires either an invitation from current maintainers, or self-nominating
to a current maintainer.

## Term

A maintainer serves for one year, renewing automatically unless they've been
inactive for 6 months before renewal or choose to step down (which they can
do at any time).
