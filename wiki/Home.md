# Welcome to the Physlib wiki

Physlib is an open-source, community project to digitalize results from
physics into [Lean 4](https://lean-lang.org/).

- 🌐 [physlib.io](https://physlib.io) — website, getting started guide, TODO list
- 💬 [Physlib Zulip](https://leanprover.zulipchat.com/#narrow/channel/479953-Physlib) — discussion and help
- 📚 [API docs](https://physlib.io/docs/)

## Project goals

- Contain results (definitions, theorems, lemmas and calculations) from
  **physics**, formalized into Lean 4.
- Be organized by physics, not by proof technique.
- Carry physics-based documentation on every definition and module.
- Stay flexible rather than tied to one axiomatization (e.g. not locked to
  axiomatic QFT).
- Be carefully reviewed and curated for reusability and readability.
- Remain fully open-source, community-run, and independent of any company.
- Stay focused on **mainstream** physics.
